How safe is it to entrust your hard earned money with HDFC Bank

On a fine day morning, I woke up to see a battery of unauthorized deductions from my savings account. There was no notification, no sms, no email whatsoever, even though my account is active for TRANSACTION ALERTS of all types. When I called up customer support line, that I managed to get through after 30 minutes of waiting, the support executive was clueless as to what the deductions were for. Not just that, they don’t even know path to resolution.

 

Silent deductions, unresponsive support function, unreachable managers, not to mention the grievance redressal cells, run by the so called RMs and Nodal Officers, that are comparable to dump terminals.  I have been following up with the bank for about more than TWO WEEKS and I haven’t received a satisfactory RESPONSE from the bank yet. If you are contemplating business with HDFC bank, be prepared for unpredictable eventualities.

Unauthorized Deductions

I requested to talk to support supervisor, though reluctant initially, the support guy put me through his manager who abruptly cut the call after few minutes of talking when he realized the deductions were unreasonable, and he will have to answer my questions and perhaps may have to agree rolling them back. I get a cold welcome when I quote the complaint reference number in the subsequent follow-ups with support team on the issue. All they could muster up as a response is that ‘an officer is working on the issue” - and yes! that is the one-liner you  ever hear from the support team or any division heads for that matter.

 

While they took less than 14 SECONDS to strip my funds, it is more that 14 WORKING DAYS since the issue started gathering dust under their ass.  As far as banking with HDFC is concerned, it works, when it works - If you get stuck on something, your happiness is royally screwed!

FOURTEEN working days, ELEVEN email follow-ups, SEVEN escalations, several phone calls, to resolve a simple query -  guess what? - I am kept hanging still.

HDFC Support Sucks!

FOURTEEN working days, ELEVEN email followups, SEVEN escalations, several (I lost count) phone calls, to resolve a simple query - and guess what - I am still kept hanging - thats HDFC support for a preferred customer - not to mention the cases for normal users and senior citizens. When I questioned  the status in an email, a head of the Team HDFC goes on record that "it is only normal for my team to take these many days to resolve an issue regardless of the priority. I cannot give you a drop-dead date as to when we are planning to resolve your problem. It may happen quickly or it may take several days. However we will TRY to resolve asap". Really HDFC?? Give me a fuc**** break!

 

A local co-operative bank in any remote village in India operates 10x faster than this modern bank. In a digital age where everyone is on the high with IMPS, Mobile Banking, RFID, instant chat support and stuff, HDFC support staff choose to push even priority cases under the carpet - You will not be heard unless you pester them relentlessly, shake their bones, hold their neck or kick their butt.

 

HDFC social forms are hopeless because HDFC seem to have automated scripts responding to your posts with stock templates. No matter you have a question, complaint or a compliment, the response will be “sorry for the inconvenience, please write to me at talktous@hdfc.com” - funny is it? Sometimes facts are stranger than fiction.

Exercise caution while dealing with HDFC - few tips

If you an HDFC Customer (banking), make sure that you:

 

  • Sign up for transaction alerts for all types: Though this not a guarantee that you will be notified of banks’ deductions.
  • Prepare to call up customer care: Yes! you read it right, reserve time exclusively for this task. You are very likely to get clogged in their support lines.  It usually takes an average of 30 minutes to cut through the call traffic. Support infrastructure is unbelievably pathetic.
  • Be on a constant watch on the transactions: Log into your account as often as you can, comb through the transactions to spot any unauthorized ones.
  • TDS Harassment: Regardless of your submission of 15G/15H, you are likely to be burdened by unauthorized cuts. Make sure to fire up your ire to customer support at the earliest.
