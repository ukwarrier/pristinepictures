about me

A passion for designing, illustrating, enhancing organizing and managing the efforts that goes into a project from start to finish - in that context, I am a project management professional (PMP), an accomplished technology writer, self-taught UX/UI designer, infinitely curious photographer and a traveler.

 

Over the years, I have acquired quite a bit of transferrable skills working on various aspects of information architecture such as content development, content quality assurance, web standards, user experience, visual design, usability and accessibility. I have contributed towards product design, website redesigns and new product launches in terms of UX support. I also have demonstrated project management experience in working with geographically distributed teams, across time zones.

 

pristinepictures.in is where I share my shots, snippets of my life, play with text and code, talk a bit about technology along with some random daily-blah-blah. I also make an attempt to spice it up a bit with an occasional product review or tutorial.

 

If you wanna talk to me about how we can work together on something meaningful, feel free to poke me :)
