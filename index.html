about me

Photography is a hobby that helps me get off my work desk. I shoot nature, culture, street life and every little amazing things that triggers my impulsive inspiration. So I have quite a bit of frozen memories from places I visited.

Designing, has always been a very important part of my life. Bringing a brand online, concepting, or stepping out to freeze some beautiful moments are all passions of mine. To cut it short, I love to see things getting built-up and always glad to learn something new.  Feel free to poke me if you have a question or two… I respond to all emails I receive :)
